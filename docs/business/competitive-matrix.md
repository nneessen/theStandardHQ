## Risk / objection handling

| Likely exec objection | Response |
|---|---|
| *"Big incumbents will copy this"* | They've had 20 years and haven't. Their tech debt blocks AI integration. AgencyZoom was acquired *because* Vertafore couldn't build it. |
| *"Vertical SaaS is a small TAM"* | US life-insurance agencies: ~30,000+ shops · IMO market: ~500. At Growth tier, $1.5k × 1,000 agencies = $18M ARR. We need <3% market share. |
| *"AI features will commoditize"* | The moat isn't the AI — it's the **insurance-specific feature engineering** (17-signal heat model, carrier acceptance DSL, commission contract logic). That takes years of domain immersion. |
| *"What if Anthropic raises prices?"* | Heat scoring tier is layered (deterministic baseline still works without AI). Voice clone is passthrough cost. Margin protected. |
