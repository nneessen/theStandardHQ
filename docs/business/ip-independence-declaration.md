# Statement of Independent Ownership — The Standard HQ Platform

**Effective Date:** May 11, 2026
**Subject:** The Standard HQ software platform ("the Platform")
**Owner:** Nick Neessen, an individual, residing in Denver, Colorado

---

## Purpose

This statement is a contemporaneous record of the facts surrounding the development, ownership, and operation of the Platform. It is maintained as evidence of the Platform's status as my independent personal property. It is intended to be produced, if necessary, in response to any future claim or assertion that the Platform is the property of, or subject to any rights of, any third party.

This is an internal document. It is not a public statement, an admission of any dispute, or a representation of any party other than the Owner.

---

## 1. Ownership

I, Nick Neessen, am the sole and exclusive owner of all right, title, and interest in and to the Platform, including without limitation all source code, object code, design, user interface, database schemas, data models, algorithms, features, workflows, documentation, branding, trademarks (whether registered or unregistered), and all related content and materials (collectively, the "Platform IP"). The Platform is my individual personal property. I have not assigned, licensed, encumbered, or otherwise transferred any rights in the Platform IP to any other person or entity.

## 2. Independent Development

I developed the Platform:

- **On my own time** — outside of any working hours owed to any employer, principal, IMO, FMO, agency, brokerage, or carrier;
- **With my own resources** — using equipment, hardware, software licenses, internet access, cloud infrastructure, domain registrations, and tooling that I personally own, lease, or pay for from my personal funds;
- **Without external funding** — no IMO, FMO, agency, brokerage, carrier, or other party contributed capital, paid me to develop the Platform, or otherwise sponsored its development;
- **Without using confidential information** — the Platform does not use, incorporate, or rely upon any confidential information, trade secrets, commission schedules, override structures, downline data, internal forms, training materials, software, or other proprietary property of any IMO, FMO, agency, brokerage, carrier, or other party with which I am or have been affiliated;
- **Using only general industry knowledge** — knowledge applied to the development of the Platform is my general skill and experience as an insurance professional, publicly available information, or my own original work.

## 3. Relationship with Founders Financial Group

I am affiliated with Founders Financial Group ("Founders") as an independent insurance agent / independent contractor. For the avoidance of doubt:

- **Founders does not own the Platform.** Founders has no ownership interest, license, beneficial interest, or other right in or to the Platform or the Platform IP.
- **Founders did not commission the Platform.** The Platform was not developed at Founders' request, direction, or expense. Founders did not pay me to develop the Platform, did not provide resources for its development, and did not contribute intellectual property to it.
- **The Platform is not a work made for hire.** The Platform was not developed within the scope of any employment or contractor relationship with Founders. It is not a "work made for hire" within the meaning of 17 U.S.C. § 101 on behalf of Founders.
- **My agreement with Founders does not assign the Platform.** No provision of any agent agreement, independent contractor agreement, non-disclosure agreement, non-solicitation agreement, non-compete agreement, or other agreement between Founders and me assigns, transfers, encumbers, or grants any rights in or to the Platform. The Platform was developed outside the scope of any such agreement.
- **The Platform does not transmit data to Founders.** The Platform does not share, transmit, or disclose user data, account data, or business data to Founders.
- **The Platform is not operated by, for, or on behalf of Founders.** Founders does not host, control, administer, sponsor, or direct the operation of the Platform.

## 4. Relationship with Self Made Financial

I am affiliated with Self Made Financial ("Self Made"), an insurance agency operating within Founders. The agents who currently use the Platform are affiliated with Self Made. For the avoidance of doubt:

- **Self Made does not own the Platform.** Self Made has no ownership interest, license, beneficial interest, or other right in or to the Platform or the Platform IP.
- **Self Made did not commission the Platform.** The Platform was not developed at Self Made's request, direction, or expense. Self Made did not pay me to develop the Platform, did not provide resources for its development, and did not contribute intellectual property to it.
- **The Platform is not Self Made's internal platform.** The Platform is my independent commercial software product. Self Made agents access the Platform from me, in their individual capacities, for their own business purposes. Their use of the Platform does not make the Platform the property of Self Made.
- **My agreement with Self Made does not assign the Platform.** No provision of any agent agreement, contractor agreement, or other agreement between Self Made and me assigns, transfers, encumbers, or grants any rights in or to the Platform. The Platform was developed outside the scope of any such agreement.
- **Use by Self Made agents does not create rights for Self Made.** The fact that multiple users of the Platform happen to share an affiliation with Self Made does not grant Self Made any ownership, license, beneficial interest, or other right in or to the Platform. Each user accepts the Platform's Terms of Service in his or her individual capacity.
- **The Platform is not operated by, for, or on behalf of Self Made.** Self Made does not host, control, administer, sponsor, or direct the operation of the Platform.

## 5. Commercial Operation

The Platform is operated by me as my independent commercial software product. I charge fees to certain users for access to the Platform or to specific features of the Platform. Revenue generated by the Platform belongs solely to me; no portion of such revenue belongs to, must be shared with, or accrues to the benefit of Founders, Self Made, or any other organization with which I am or have been affiliated.

## 6. Brand Independence

"The Standard HQ" is my product name for the Platform. The Platform's name is distinct from, and does not refer to, Founders, Self Made, or any other agency, brokerage, or organization. Where the Platform is described in connection with a specific agency, that description is for context only and does not transfer ownership of the Platform to that agency.

I maintain the following contemporaneous evidence of independent development and ownership:

- Source code version history (git commit timestamps and authorship) demonstrating the development timeline and that I am the sole developer;
- Hosting, infrastructure, and software-license invoices in my personal name;
- Domain registration(s) in my personal name;

These records are available for inspection if necessary to substantiate the facts stated above.
