Common Questions
Q "Aren't annuities complicated and full of hidden fees?"
Some are — but fixed indexed annuities are among the simpler structures in retirement planning. Our specialists only work with products where the fee structure is fully transparent and the tradeoffs are clearly explained before you commit to anything.
Q "Is this only for people with a lot of money?"
Not at all. These strategies are often most valuable for people with modest retirement savings — the ones who can't afford to lose a significant portion to a market downturn. There's no minimum net worth required for a consultation.
Q "What if I need access to the money?"
Liquidity varies by product, and that's exactly what gets reviewed in the consultation. Many strategies include penalty-free withdrawal options after a set period, and IBC structures are specifically designed to give you access to your own capital whenever you need it.
Q "How is this different from what my 401(k) is doing?"
Your 401(k) is designed for accumulation — it grows by taking on market risk. These strategies are designed for distribution. They protect what you've built so you can draw a reliable income without worrying about what the S&P did last quarter.
